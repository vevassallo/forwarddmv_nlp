⚡ The Paradox of Optimism
The most striking finding across all 95 respondents: professionals simultaneously believe AI will make the future "better" (avg. 3.4/5) and that it will replace more jobs than it creates (avg. 1.4/5). This isn't contradiction - it's a vision of a future that's more efficient and wealthier in aggregate, but structurally worse for the average worker. Workforce Development professionals, the very people tasked with placing talent, were the most pessimistic on job creation - a signal the region cannot ignore.

Strategic Initiatives
ForwardDMV's concrete actions launching to address the challenges discussed. Focused on talent transition, early-career pathways, and employer coordination.

Gig Economy Accelerator
Spring 2026. Short-term, project-based work for displaced federal experts - "solopreneurs" not gig drivers. Includes vetted platform, shared admin services (legal, tax, contracts), mentorship, and a "teaming exchange" connecting niche talent to prime contractors.
